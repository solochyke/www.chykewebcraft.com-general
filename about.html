Meet the Founder, Solomon Chyke

Entrepreneurship is more than a career for me—it’s a family tradition. I grew up witnessing the discipline and precision required to run Chyke Global Petroleum, a company that operated on an international scale from Nigeria to the UK. That environment taught me that in business, reliability and performance are the only metrics that matter.

I founded Chyke Web Craft Agency (CWCA) to bring that same "Global" standard to the digital world. I saw too many talented professionals being held back by mediocre websites that didn't reflect their true value. My background in high-stakes commerce drives me to build systems that aren't just beautiful, but are engineered for growth and lead generation.
